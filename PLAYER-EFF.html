Its important to note that EFF is not a perfect metric and has limitations. It does not capture certain aspects of the game, such as a players defensive impact, intangibles like leadership, or the quality of opposition faced. Therefore, while EFF can provide valuable insights into a players performance, it should be considered alongside other metrics and qualitative observations to form a comprehensive assessment.
